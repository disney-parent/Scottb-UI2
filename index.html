Disney Parent App!
Are you tired of missing out on the rides you love when you go on your Disney trip?
Introducing the Disney Parent App! With Disney Parent App you can find parents or locate one of our trusted childcare organizations at the park to swap childcare or stroller passes with. Add some pixie dust to your Disney Experience by using the Disney Parent App and go on all the rides you've been missing out on!

Signing Up is A Breeze

Sign up quick and easily by downloading the Disney Parent App!

Meet Swap Buddies With The Push Of A Button

From the menu you can make a request, or view other requests, enabling you to swiftly meet up with another parent or drop off your little prince or princess with one of our childcare organizations.

Go Enjoy The Rides You Would Normally Sit Out!

Once your request is answered, it is time to go on that solo or couple adventure, fly like Peter Pan over to your destination and have a magical time!
